Mixology

Our events are designed for all levels of patrons - those that want to learn more about alcohol or no-alcohol ingredients, how they are paired with each other, alongside other ingredients such as homemade, local, rare or even alchemy like altered / processed ingredients.

Cocktail connoisseurs will be delighted to able to taste their favorite classics, listen to bar lore from real experienced drink slingers, but most importantly we are also more than willing to create a custom class, cocktail or experience for you based on the types of drinks you’d like to focus on and the level of instruction you and your group require.

Some people want to learn it all and impress their friends, whereas the others just want to sit back and enjoy their simple, yet flawless drink in a private environment. We relate to both sides.
